Our process

How We Work With You

Step 1: R&D Eligibility Assessment

We evaluate your project’s technical objectives, activities, and intent to determine fit under current ATO rules.

Step 2: Financial and Technical Review

We collect records of time, costs, experiments, and other supporting materials to establish a strong claim base.

Step 3: Claim Preparation and Submission

We prepare compliant AusIndustry registrations and lodge your claim via the ATO, ensuring all deadlines are met.

Step 4: Audit-Ready Support

We maintain your documentation and defend claims during reviews or audits, minimising business disruption.

Step 5: Additional Grant Opportunities

Our team identifies other innovation-related programs, rebates, and sector grants that support your ongoing R&D.
