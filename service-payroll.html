Our process

Our Payroll Workflow

1. Employee Information Collection

We gather and verify names, Tax File Numbers, award details, and pay rates.

2. Time Tracking and Attendance

We set up or sync with systems that record hours, shifts, leave, and allowances.

3. Payroll Calculation

We calculate wages, deductions, allowances, overtime, and super contributions.

4. Payroll Approval

We present payroll summaries for your approval before payments are processed.

5. Payment Distribution

Employee payments and payslips are issued securely and on time.

6. Tax Filing and Compliance

We lodge STP, PAYG, and superannuation reports with ATO on schedule.

7. Recordkeeping and Reporting

Complete payroll records, reports, and audit trails are securely stored and accessible.
