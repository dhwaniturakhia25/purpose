Our process

Our SMSF Service Process

Step 1: SMSF Setup and Registration

We help establish your SMSF with the ATO, set up bank accounts, and create trust deeds.

Step 2: Accounting & Investment Recording

Using Class Super, we record contributions, investments, and fund activity in real time.

Step 3: Annual Tax & Audit Coordination

We prepare financial statements, coordinate independent audits, and lodge your SMSF annual return.

Step 4: Pension Planning & Ongoing Support

We provide guidance for moving to pension phase and ensure income is drawn tax-effectively.

Common Scenarios

  • Setting up a new SMSF to invest in commercial or residential property
  • Transitioning into pension phase for tax-free income access
  • Managing contributions and rollovers from other funds
  • Maintaining compliance for existing SMSFs
